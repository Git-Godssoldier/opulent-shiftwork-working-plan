Opulent Technical Capabilities: Jeremy Alston demonstrated the Opulent interface, highlighting its ability to simulate complex systems like ERP environments (00:12:40). This strategy optimizes token usage and significantly reduces costs compared to using high-end models for every task (00:19:09).
Knowledge Aggregation and System Evolution: Jeremy Alston highlighted the platform’s "dreams in memory" feature, which recursively saves snippets from tool calls to update knowledge bases and runbooks (00:26:56).
